Consultório Live - Software Médico para Clínicas e Consultórios

## Company and Product Description
Consultório Live is a complete system developed by VBB Software Ltda (in business since 2004) designed to simplify and optimize the routine of clinics and offices of all specialties. It is a cloud-based solution (AWS) accessible from any platform (Windows, Mac, Android, iOS) without installation, focusing on productivity and security (SSL 256-bit, LGPD compliant).

## Main Features
### Patients & Records
- **Detailed Electronic Health Record (EHR):** Customizable templates for anamnesis, certificates, and declarations.
- **Digital Prescription:** Integrated with Memed for electronic prescriptions with digital signature.
- **Teleconsultation:** Remote video appointments integrated into the system.
- **History:** Timeline view of previous consultations, attachments (images/files), and odontogram.
- **AI Summary:** Intelligent summaries of patient evolution.

### Scheduling & Management
- **Flexible Agenda:** Customizable hours per professional, repeat bookings, and "gap" filling (encaixes).
- **Online Booking:** Patients can book appointments directly via a customizable website.
- **Confirmation System:** Automated confirmations via WhatsApp, SMS, and Email to reduce no-shows.
- **Waiting Room:** Control panel with call-by-voice feature for waiting patients.

### Financial & Administrative
- **Complete Financial Module:** Cash flow, accounts payable/receivable, and professional commissions.
- **TISS Billing:** Issuance of consultation guides (SP/SADT) in XML format for health insurance plans.
- **Inventory Control:** Management of batches, expiration dates, and sales.
- **Marketing:** Website generator, automated birthday emails, and MailChimp integration.

### Technical Details
- **Mobile Apps:** Official apps for Android (Google Play) and iOS (App Store).
- **Security:** Daily backups, 2-factor authentication, and API for custom integrations.
