## About TuoTempo

Founded in 2006 and part of the Docplanner Group since 2019, TuoTempo is an advanced CRM designed for healthcare providers, including hospitals, polyclinics, diagnostic centers, health insurance companies. 
As a global leader as Software as a Service in the field, we provide modular digital solutions for efficient patient management, operational processes, and healthcare marketing. 

Our mission is to make the relationship between clinics, hospitals, and patients simpler and more immediate, reducing management costs and improving patient satisfaction.
