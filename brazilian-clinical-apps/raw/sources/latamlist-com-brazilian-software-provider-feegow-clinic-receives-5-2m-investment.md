## Brazilian software provider Feegow Clinic receives $5.2M investment

- byCiara Middleton
- July 5, 2019
- 1 minute read    0Shares                     
LatAm List &#8211; DNA Capital, a healthcare fund in Latin America, has invested approximately $5.2M (R$20M) in Feegow Clinic, a Brazilian software provider, that offers an online management system for medical centers. 

DNA Capital has partnered with Feegow Clinic to help stimulate the company’s plans for future expansion. 

&#8220;The partnership with DNA Capital through the investment made in Feegow is paramount for the growth we look forward to seeing in the coming months. Our staff is increasing, we are expanding our service potential, and we understand that we must accelerate to achieve our desired goals,” explained Silvio Maia, Feegow’s CEO, to Baguete.
