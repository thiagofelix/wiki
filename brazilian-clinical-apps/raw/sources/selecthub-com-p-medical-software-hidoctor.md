## Researcher's Summary:

Is HiDoctor just what the doctor ordered, or will it leave you feeling sick? User reviews from the last year paint a somewhat mixed picture of this comprehensive medical software solution. While many appreciate its intuitive interface and robust features like electronic medical records (EMR), e-prescribing, and appointment scheduling, some have pointed out areas that could use improvement. 

HiDoctor's ability to streamline clinical workflows and reduce data entry time through its integration with existing medical devices is a major selling point for many users. One user praised the software's customization options, stating that the ability to tailor templates and workflows to their specific needs was "a major plus." Another highlighted the software's offline functionality as a game-changer, allowing them to access patient records and other critical information even without an internet connection. However, some users have reported experiencing occasional glitches and a complex setup process. 

Overall, HiDoctor seems best suited for clinics and doctor's offices that need a robust, all-in-one EMR solution and can navigate a potentially bumpy setup process. Its offline capabilities make it particularly appealing for practices in areas with unreliable internet connectivity. However, potential users should carefully weigh the pros and cons, perhaps even "consulting" with existing users, before committing to this software.
