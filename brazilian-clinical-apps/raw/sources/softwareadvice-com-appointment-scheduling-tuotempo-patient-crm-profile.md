## Product Description
TuoTempo Patient CRM is a patient scheduling solution designed for healthcare providers of any size and specialty. It can be deployed as an on-premise or cloud-based solution. The system is capable of automating several tasks related to patient communication and scheduling, including:
- Appointment confirmations, reminders, and cancellations.
- Test result notifications and post-appointment satisfaction surveys.
- Notifications explaining delays in the doctor's schedule and post-appointment follow-ups.
- Cloud-based appointment booking with online payment collection.
- Integration with major Electronic Health Records (EHR) and Practice Management systems to reduce costs and build patient loyalty ([Software Advice](https://www.softwareadvice.com/appointment-scheduling/tuotempo-patient-crm-profile/)).
