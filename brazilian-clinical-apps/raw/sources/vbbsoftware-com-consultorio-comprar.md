The page for Consultório Desktop by [VBB Software](https://www.vbbsoftware.com/) offers several software licensing plans for clinics and offices.

### Page Title
Escolha o melhor plano para sua clínica ou consultório.

### Company and Product Description
[VBB Software](https://www.vbbsoftware.com/) develops useful, functional, and easy-to-use systems to streamline daily routines. [Consultório Desktop](https://www.vbbsoftware.com/consultorio/) is described as a complete, practical, and easy tool for professionals looking to improve their clinic's administrative department.

### Plans and Pricing
All plans are annual licenses with a minimum 12-month payment requirement. A 10% discount is available for upfront payments via PIX or bank slip (boleto).

*   **Básico:** R$ 48/month - License for 1 computer. Includes free SMS sending, mobile calendar access, remote assistance, and constant updates.
*   **Clássico:** R$ 61/month - License for 2 computers. Includes all Basic features plus Network Sharing.
*   **Dinâmico:** R$ 81/month - License for 4 computers. Includes all Classic features.
*   **Premium:** R$ 127/month - License for 8 computers. Includes all Classic features.

### Key Features
*   Free SMS sending (Envio de SMS gratuito)
*   Mobile calendar access (Acesso à agenda via celular)
*   Remote technical assistance (Assistência Remota)
*   Constant updates (Atualizações constantes)
*   Network sharing (Compartilhamento em Rede) — available in Clássico, Dinâmico, and Premium plans.
