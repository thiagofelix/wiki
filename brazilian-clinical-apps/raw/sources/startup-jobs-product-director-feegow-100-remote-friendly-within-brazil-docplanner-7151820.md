Job Title: Product Director, Feegow (100% remote-friendly within Brazil)
Company: Docplanner
Location: Brazil (100% remote-friendly)
Type: Full-Time
Salary: R$37.000 – R$44.500 per month

TLDR:
Lead product teams at Feegow, driving innovation and modernization as a critical player's effort in expanding the Practice Management System market in Brazil.

About Feegow and Docplanner:
Feegow is the leading Practice Management System (PMS) in Brazil for individual doctors & large clinics alike, and a highly strategic product for Docplanner Group, as a foundation to conquer the Brazilian market. Docplanner builds digital healthcare platforms that connect patients with healthcare professionals, enhancing the medical experience across Europe and Latin America. Their innovative solutions focus on patient engagement, making healthcare more accessible and personalized. With a strong presence in key markets, Docplanner is dedicated to transforming how healthcare is delivered and experienced.

Job Description:
We are looking for a passionate and experienced Director of Product for Feegow to lead our product teams, drive innovation, accountability, and play a key role in our product's modernization. This is a remote-friendly role within Brazil, where you’ll foster collaboration between global teams, steer product development, and manage a talented product team. Your expertise in building a data-driven & business minded product culture whilst delivering fast & safe iterations, knowing when to go for PoCs & robust solutions based on the business appetite and product maturity will ensure the continued growth of Feegow and Docplanner Group.

What we expect from this position:
• Inspire, lead and bring in PMS product vision to all product managers and teams. 
• Understand a complex environment where the teams have not only individual doctors and clinics that should be delighted with their Efficiency platform, whilst bringing them new value propositions like Visibility & Growth, as well as betting on AI as a door opener for exponential value.
• Define with PMs the product strategy on every team, how it relates to the broader Brazilian strategy & Docplanner Tech & Product strategy.
• Influence and establish a shared vision with other business areas as Sales, Operations and Marketing. Be a key stakeholder for Individuals and Clinics Business owners.
• Ensure upcoming product needs and opportunities aligned with your Engineering Director and Country Managers to create a coherent Product & Tech Strategy and Roadmap.
• Champion a hands-on approach to innovation by using a combination of Data, listening to the market, and “getting out of the building” to get first-hand customer/user insights.
• Create a culture of decision-making based on insight, analysis and data across the business.
• Work closely with our Global VP Product, on standardizing the objectives in clear indicators & tasks and bring transparency on ‘the plan’ across the whole company.

Requirements:
• Minimum 3 years’ work experience as Product Director / GPM (managing and coaching a group of Product Managers)
• Experience on B2B SaaS scale-ups
• Solid experience in fast high-growth environment, change management, & business growth
• A proven track record of delivering significant success, scaling and developing high performing digital products that impact business & delight customers
• Expertise in the end-to-end product development lifecycle, agile and lean software development practices
• An entrepreneurial mindset with outstanding organizational, communicative and leadership skills
• Business savvy, holistic business acumen outside of Product Management and ability to assess product market fit 
• Analytical abilities and problem-solving skills
• Experience in healthcare is a plus not a must
• Experience in modernizing tech debt whilst delivering product value is a plus
• Hands-on: In Docplanner, being a people manager does not mean only managing people, but being able to dive in the details of problems, opportunities & solutions.
