## Pixeon

			        https://www.pixeon.com/

Pixeon is a provider of software addressing the imaging and workflow needs of healthcare providers in Brazil.  The Company provides picture archiving and communications systems (PACS) for medical image storage and workflow, Radiology Information Systems / Laboratory Information Systems (RIS/LIS) managing the entire operational cycle for labs and clinics; and Hospital Information Systems (HIS) / Electronic Medical Records (EMR) to manage workflow and patient data within hospitals.

Investment Overview

In late 2013, Riverwood invested in Pixeon to fund an aggressive growth initiative and to consolidate Pixeon&#8217;s market position in Brazil and across Latin America.
