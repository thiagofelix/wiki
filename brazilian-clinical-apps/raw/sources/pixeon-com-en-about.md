## 
                        Our history                    

## 
                                            The birth of Pixeon                                        

                                            Our story began with the union of visionary companies that decided to transform healthcare through technology. Pixeon was born, guided by efficiency, security, and a strong commitment to results.                                        

                                        2003 &#8211; 2012                                    

## 
                                            Technology that expands boundaries                                        

                                            We continue to grow with strategic investments and acquisitions that strengthen our portfolio. The integration of solutions such as MedicWare, LabLink, and Digitalmed has established Pixeon as one of the leading healthtech companies in Brazil and Latin America.                                        

                                        2013 – 2017                                    

## 
                                            Connecting the entire healthcare journey                                        

                                            We further expanded our reach with the arrival of BoaConsulta, becoming part of the Vela LatAm group. This reinforced our position as one of the top healthtech companies in Latin America. We continue connecting every stage of the healthcare journey — from care delivery to management — launching new solutions while maintaining our commitment to efficiency and excellence in every interaction.                                        

                                        2018 – 2025
