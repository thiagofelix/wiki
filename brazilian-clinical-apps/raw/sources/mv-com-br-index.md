Source: https://mv.com.br

The website for [MV](https://mv.com.br) features technology for 360° healthcare management.

**Page Title:** MV Saúde Digital

**Company and Product Description:**
MV provides technology aimed at increasing productivity, humanizing care, and achieving better results through a "360° Management in Health" approach. Their platform offers specialized control for business management, particularly for health plan operators, organizing administrative, financial, and managerial processes while simplifying contract and beneficiary management, audit of accounts, and procedure authorizations.

**Key Solutions:**
- **SOUL MV Operadora:** Specialized business management for health plan operators.
- **Global Health:** Digital health platform.
- **Gestão Estratégica e Qualidade:** Strategic management and quality control.
- **VIVACE MV/mPACS:** Diagnostic imaging and RIS/PACS solutions.
- **SOUL MV Hospitalar:** Management system for hospitals.
