## 1. Amplimed

**Website:** https://www.amplimed.com.br  
**Category:** Clinic Management / Health Ecosystem Hub

### Company Overview

Amplimed describes itself as a "hub de conexão da saúde" (healthcare connection hub) that connects health professionals, clinics, laboratories, pharmacies, and health plan operators. The platform focuses on clinic and consultório management with a comprehensive electronic health record (EHR), AI-assisted transcription, telemedicine, and TISS billing. As of its latest public metrics, Amplimed serves **70,000+ health professionals** and more than **51 million patients**, with over **1.9 million appointments** processed.

### Founding Year

- **Founded:** 2014 (LinkedIn/Prospeo); 2015 is cited by CBInsights, likely reflecting an incorporation or product launch date.
- **Parent/Acquirer:** Acquired by **Raia Drogasil (RD Group)** in December 2021 (100% of Amplisoftware Tecnologia, trading as Amplimed). The acquisition was approved by the General Meeting on November 18, 2021 and confirmed by CADE.

### Headquarters

- **Chapecó, Santa Catarina, Brazil**
- Address: Av. Getúlio Dorneles Vargas 1183n, 12º andar, Chapecó, SC 89802-002

### Founders

Not publicly disclosed in available sources; prior investors include **Endeavor Scale-Up Brazil** and **Startup SC** (Santa Catarina state accelerator). The company operated independently before acquisition by Raia Drogasil.

### Funding / Investors

| Round | Investor(s) | Notes |
|-------|------------|-------|
| Seed / Accelerator | Startup SC, Endeavor Scale-Up Brazil | Early-stage support |
| Acquisition | Raia Drogasil (RD Group) | 100% acquisition, December 2021; no disclosed price |

Raia Drogasil is Brazil's largest pharmacy chain (revenue > R$ 30.9 billion in 2022, ~2,697 stores), and acquired Amplimed as part of its digital health ecosystem strategy alongside Vitat, Healthbit, and Cuco Health.

Sources: [CBInsights](https://www.cbinsights.com/company/amplimedcombr), [Cadernos EBAPE.BR/SciELO](https://www.scielo.br/j/cebape/a/qm9rwQQrLvbyHjhTmYXLWFc/?lang=en), [Legal 500 – RD Group GC](https://www.legal500.com/gc-powerlist/brazil-2023/elton-da-silva-de-oliveira/)

## 2. Feegow

**Website:** https://feegowclinic.com.br (primary product site); https://www.feegow.com (corporate/redirect)  
**Category:** Practice Management System (PMS) / Clinic Management ERP

### Company Overview

Feegow is Brazil's leading Practice Management System for clinics and individual practitioners. Founded in 2009 by **Silvio Maia**, it grew into a comprehensive, cloud-based clinic management platform with 200+ features. As of the 2022 DocPlanner acquisition, Feegow served **40,000+ healthcare professionals** and **68,000+ total users** (including receptionists, financial analysts, and managers). The platform is now operated under the **DocPlanner Group** (Poland), Brazil's largest digital health marketplace operator.

### Founding Year

- **2009**, founded by Silvio Maia in Rio de Janeiro.

### Headquarters

- **Rio de Janeiro, RJ, Brazil**
- Address: Rua Equador 43, Bloco 003, Salas 1201–1205, Santo Cristo, Rio de Janeiro – RJ 20220-410

Also operates from **Curitiba, PR** (hybrid model office).

### Founders

- **Silvio Maia** — Founder and CEO (continued as CEO post-acquisition)

### Funding / Investors

| Round | Amount | Investor(s) | Date |
|-------|--------|------------|------|
| Series A | ~R$ 20M (~USD 5.2M) | **DNA Capital** | July 2019 |
| Follow-on | Undisclosed | **DNA Capital** | May 2020 |
| **Acquisition** | Undisclosed | **DocPlanner Group** | July 2022 |

- **DNA Capital** is a global, healthcare-focused investment firm with offices in São Paulo and San Francisco. It led the Series A in 2019 and followed with additional investment in 2020. [Source: LatamList, July 2019](https://latamlist.com/brazilian-software-provider-feegow-clinic-receives-5-2m-investment/)

### Company Overview

ProDoctor is one of Brazil's longest-running medical software companies, entering the market in **1993**. It serves **65,000+ clients** across all Brazilian states, with an NPS of 97 for customer support satisfaction. The platform offers tiered products from simple consultório management (ProDoctor Cloud) to enterprise-level clinic and hospital systems (ProDoctor Corp), with AI-powered transcription, open API, and TISS/TUSS compliance.
