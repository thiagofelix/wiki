## 1. iClinic / Afya iClinic

### Company Overview

iClinic is Brazil's leading cloud-based clinic-management software for independent physicians and small-to-medium clinics. Originally founded to give private-practice doctors the same technology available to large hospitals, it grew into a comprehensive SaaS platform covering scheduling, electronic health records (EHR), telemedicine, financial management, and patient marketing. The company was acquired by **Afya Limited** in January 2021 and now operates under the brand **Afya iClinic** as part of Afya's physician digital products division.

| **Headquarters** | Ribeirão Preto, São Paulo, Brazil |
| **Current parent** | Afya Limited (NASDAQ: AFYA) |
| **Website** | [iclinic.com.br](https://iclinic.com.br) |

### Founders

- **Felipe Lourenço** – Co-founder & CEO (through the acquisition); graduated in Biomedical Informatics, USP Ribeirão Preto. Now Director of Digital Medical Products at Afya. ([Source – Projeto Draft](https://www.projetodraft.com/a-iclinic-conecta-medicos-e-pacientes-com-um-modulo-de-telemedicina/))
- **Leonardo Berdu** – Co-founder

### Funding & Investors

### Key Features

- **Prontuário Eletrônico (EHR):** Fully customizable per specialty; patient history, exam attachments, photo/image comparison, calculators, digital signature, electronic prescription (iClinic Rx with up-to-date drug database sent via WhatsApp)
- **Agenda Médica:** Color-coded appointment types, auto-reminders (SMS, email, WhatsApp), waitlist management, recurring appointments, Google/Apple Calendar sync, online booking via AgendarConsulta.com
- **Telemedicina (Teleconsulta):** Integrated video consultations with in-session EHR access, screen sharing, session recording, consent forms, unlimited on the Premium plan
- **Gestão Financeira:** Cash flow automation, expense/revenue control, TISS-standard health-plan billing guides, medical repasse, multi-bank accounts
- **Marketing Médico:** Email campaign tools, patient segmentation by age/gender/record tags, anniversary messages
- **Controle de Estoque:** Stock management integrated with financial module
- **AgendarConsulta Marketplace:** Free professional profile on the patient-facing booking portal

### Target Market

Independent physicians, dentists, psychologists, physiotherapists, and other health professionals; more than 50 specialties; 750+ cities across Brazil. Primary focus is the **independent private practice** (consultório/clínica) segment, not hospitals.

> *"Ajudamos mais de 22 mil profissionais, de 53 especialidades"* — iClinic blog, 2020. Since the Afya acquisition the platform has continued to grow.

30-day free trial and 30-day money-back guarantee available. Pricing is per professional; secretaries and admin users are unlimited on all plans.

### Key Integrations

- **Telemedicine:** Fully integrated native video module
- **Payments:** Financial module with bank account reconciliation; not a payment gateway per se
- **AgendarConsulta:** Patient-facing booking marketplace; embeddable on clinic websites
- **iClinic Rx:** Electronic prescription module integrated with WhatsApp delivery
- **Google / Apple Calendar:** Two-way sync
- **Whitebook:** Clinical decision-support reference (Premium plan)

### Tech Stack (Known)

Pure cloud (SaaS); no on-premise option. Accessible from any device via browser; mobile apps available for iOS and Android. LGPD-compliant data storage. Specific underlying infrastructure not publicly disclosed.

## 2. Easy Health / Easyhealth (Essentia Technologies)

### Disambiguation Note

There are multiple companies using the "Easy Health" name globally. The Brazilian clinical SaaS platform in scope is **Easy Health**, developed by **Essentia Technologies**, a company within the **Essentia Group** (Brazil). This is distinct from the US Medicare-focused EasyHealth (easyhealth.com) and the Italian easyhealth.tech.

### Company Overview

Easy Health is a cloud-based clinical-management platform developed by **Essentia Technologies**, positioned as more than an EHR — it serves as a full clinical-management assistant for doctors (especially physicians practicing **integrative medicine**) and nutritionists. It is integrated with the Essentia Group's pharmacy and laboratory ecosystem (Essentia Farma), offering laboratory exam extraction, integrative medicine references, AI-powered documentation, and a patient-facing companion app (Easy Patient).

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Product name** | Easy Health |
| **Developer** | Essentia Technologies (part of Essentia Group) |
| **Country** | Brazil |
| **Website** | [essentiatechnologies.com.br](https://essentiatechnologies.com.br) |

### Key Person

- **Fernando Rodrigues** – Director of Artificial Intelligence at Essentia Group, identified as the **idealizer of Easy Health**; postgraduate in Machine Learning; MIT-certified AI specialist in health applications. ([Source – essentiatechnologies.com.br](https://essentiatechnologies.com.br/evolutionofcare-ia/))

### Funding & Investors

No publicly disclosed funding rounds or institutional investors identified in available sources. Essentia Group appears to be privately held.

### Key Features

- **Prontuário Eletrônico:** Customizable EHR with templates for prescriptions, dietary plans, and exam orders
- **Gestão Clínica:** Appointment scheduling (intelligent agenda with reminders for doctor and patient), multi-professional simultaneous access, stock control integrated with injectable-order management
- **Integração com Essentia Farma:** Automatic extraction of laboratory exam results with graphical analysis; references from integrative medicine; creation of custom clinical indicators
- **Assinatura Digital:** Digital certificate-based signing of documents
- **Módulo Financeiro:** Financial and stock control integrated with product ordering
- **Conteúdo Educativo:** Exclusive health and wellness content, including Harvard materials translated into Portuguese
- **Easy Patient App:** Patient-facing mobile app that stores all health documents; push notifications to reduce no-shows and improve treatment adherence; strengthens the prescriber-patient relationship
- **IA (Artificial Intelligence):** AI-driven plan (as of 2024–2025) for clinical documentation transcription and predictive analytics
- Free 10-day trial available

### Target Market

Primarily **physicians** and **nutritionists** in private practice; strong focus on integrative medicine practitioners. The Essentia Farma integration suggests particular relevance for clinics that also prescribe or dispense integrative/functional medicine compounds. ([YouTube demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qM2fumCmTcM))

### Pricing Model

Subscription-based SaaS; specific published pricing not publicly available in indexed sources. A 10-day free trial is offered.

### Key Integrations

- **Essentia Farma:** Deep native integration for lab exam extraction and compounding pharmacy workflows
- **Digital Certificate Signature:** Integrated with digital certificate infrastructure
- **Telemedicine:** Not explicitly confirmed in available sources
- **TISS:** Not confirmed in available sources

### Tech Stack

Cloud-based SaaS; mobile app (Easy Patient) for patient side. Specific infrastructure not publicly disclosed. AI layer mentioned for the advanced plan (IA module for clinical documentation).

### Notable News

- Active product development ongoing as of early 2026 based on Instagram and Facebook content updates.
- Essentia Group positions Easy Health as an AI-forward platform, with Fernando Rodrigues (an MIT-certified AI specialist) cited as the product's ideator.

## 3. Shosp

### Company Overview

Shosp is a Brazilian cloud-based medical software platform focused on clinic and consultório management. Founded in Goiás in 2013, it later relocated to Rio de Janeiro through the StartupRio government innovation program. Its mission is to transform Brazil's disease-management system into a qualified healthcare system. Shosp is notable for its **TISS billing**, telemedicine, and multi-specialty support, and was recognized as one of the ten most promising Brazilian startups for 2021 by Capterra.

## 4. Doctoralia / DocPlanner Group

### Company Overview

Doctoralia is the Brazilian brand of **DocPlanner Group**, the world's largest healthcare appointment booking platform. DocPlanner was founded in 2012 in Poland by Mariusz Gralewski and merged with Spain's Doctoralia in 2016. The Brazilian operation (headquartered in Curitiba) launched in 2017 and has since become the leading health marketplace in Brazil, serving both a patient-facing booking portal and a SaaS suite for individual doctors and clinics. In July 2022, Doctoralia acquired Brazilian clinic-management SaaS **Feegow**, significantly expanding its B2B product offering.

## 5. Clínica nas Nuvens (Bionexo)

## 6. Pixeon

Pixeon is one of Brazil's leading healthcare IT companies, offering an integrated portfolio covering the full spectrum of hospital, clinic, and diagnostic-center software: HIS (Hospital Information Systems), RIS (Radiology Information Systems), PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication Systems), LIS (Laboratory Information Systems), and CIS (Clinical Information Systems). Founded in Florianópolis in 2003, Pixeon reached its current form through a series of mergers and acquisitions. ## 7. MV Informática / MV Sistemas (MV Saúde Digital)

### Company Overview

MV (formerly MV Informática / MV Sistemas) is Brazil's dominant hospital information system provider and one of the largest healthcare IT companies in Latin America. Founded in 1987, MV built the first integrated hospital management software in Brazil and introduced the country's first 100% web-based HIS (SOUL MV). It is headquartered in Recife, Pernambuco — one of Brazil's leading medical poles — and operates in 11 Latin American countries plus Africa. As of 2025, MV has positioned itself as the **5th largest hospital software provider in the world** and announced a partnership to enter the US market.
