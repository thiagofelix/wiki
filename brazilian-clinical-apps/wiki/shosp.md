Shosp is a Brazilian cloud-based medical software platform focused on clinic and consultório management. Founded in 2013 in Goiás, it later relocated to Rio de Janeiro through the StartupRio government innovation program. Its mission is to transform Brazil's disease-management system into a qualified healthcare system. Shosp is notable for its [[tiss]] billing support, integrated telemedicine, multi-specialty coverage, and a freemium entry tier — making it accessible to solo practitioners as well as small-to-medium clinic networks. It was recognized as one of Brazil's ten most promising startups for 2021 by Capterra. In the competitive landscape covered by the [[market-overview]], Shosp competes with [[iclinic]] and [[doctoralia]] in the independent-practice segment, and with [[clinica-nas-nuvens]] for growing clinic networks.

## Target Market

Small consultórios (solo practitioners), multi-specialty clinics, and multi-unit clinic networks. The platform served 5,000+ healthcare professionals as of 2021 across all major health specialties. The freemium Free plan makes Shosp a viable entry point for practitioners who cannot commit to the R$ 149+ monthly plans offered by [[iclinic]] or [[clinica-nas-nuvens]].

Source: [Shosp About Page](https://www.shosp.com.br/funcionalidades/quem-somos)

## Pricing

Subscription SaaS; priced per health professional (prestador). Annual billing available at a 15% discount. Standard contract fidelity of 12 months; early cancellation incurs an activation fee. Devices and users are unlimited across all plans.

| Plan | Monthly Price (BRL) | Key Inclusions |
|------|---------------------|----------------|
| **Free** | R$ 0 (R$ 299 activation fee) | Agenda, waitlist, patient registration, WhatsApp integration, mobile app, SSL, chat support; up to 5 users |
| **Fellowship** | R$ 149 | Free + unlimited users, multi-clinic, online scheduling, EHR, teleconsulta, prescription, data encryption, financial control, [[tiss]] billing, automated reception, digital signatures (Soluti + VIDaaS), patient relations, Flex B.I. spreadsheets |
| **Excellence** | R$ 229 | Fellowship + intelligent automated patient relationship management, open API, 100 free credits/prestador, full medical repasse, cost center, campaign scheduling, subscription plans, free unrecorded telemedicine, automated WhatsApp, Flex B.I. charts |

Source: [shosp.com.br/precos](https://www.shosp.com.br/precos), [shosp.com.br/comparativo](https://www.shosp.com.br/comparativo)
