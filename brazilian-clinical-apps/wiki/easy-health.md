Easy Health is a cloud-based clinical-management platform developed by **Essentia Technologies**, a company within the Brazilian **Essentia Group**. Positioned as more than an EHR — it serves as a full clinical-management assistant — the platform has a distinctive focus on integrative medicine practitioners and nutritionists, and integrates natively with the Essentia Group's pharmacy and laboratory ecosystem (Essentia Farma). It also offers an AI-powered documentation module and a patient-facing companion app (Easy Patient). Among Brazilian clinical platforms, Easy Health occupies a niche segment that differs from broader-market tools like [[iclinic]] or [[shosp]], targeting practitioners who prescribe or consult on integrative/functional medicine compounds. See the [[market-overview]] for competitive context.

> **Disambiguation:** Multiple companies use the "Easy Health" name globally. The Brazilian clinical SaaS in scope here is Easy Health by Essentia Technologies (essentiatechnologies.com.br), distinct from the US Medicare-focused EasyHealth (easyhealth.com) and the Italian easyhealth.tech.

## Key Features

- **Prontuário Eletrônico (EHR):** Customizable EHR with templates for prescriptions, dietary plans, and exam orders
- **Gestão Clínica:** Appointment scheduling with an intelligent agenda, reminders for both doctor and patient, multi-professional simultaneous access
- **Controle de Estoque:** Stock control integrated with injectable-order management
- **Integração com Essentia Farma:** Automatic extraction of laboratory exam results with graphical analysis; references from integrative medicine; creation of custom clinical indicators — a key differentiator from competitors like [[iclinic]] or [[shosp]]
- **Assinatura Digital:** Digital certificate-based signing of documents
- **Módulo Financeiro:** Financial and stock control integrated with product ordering
- **Conteúdo Educativo:** Exclusive health and wellness content, including Harvard materials translated into Portuguese
- **Easy Patient App:** Patient-facing mobile app that stores all health documents, sends push notifications to reduce no-shows, and strengthens the prescriber-patient relationship
- **IA (Artificial Intelligence):** AI-driven plan for clinical documentation transcription and predictive analytics (launched 2024–2025)
- 10-day free trial available

## Target Market

Primarily **physicians** and **nutritionists** in private practice, with a strong focus on **integrative and functional medicine** practitioners. The native Essentia Farma integration makes the platform particularly relevant for clinics that also prescribe or dispense integrative/functional medicine compounded preparations.

Unlike [[iclinic]] (general private-practice physicians across all specialties) or [[clinica-nas-nuvens]] (medium/large multi-specialty clinics), Easy Health targets a specialist niche within the private-practice segment.
