Doctoralia is the Brazilian brand of **DocPlanner Group**, the world's largest healthcare appointment booking platform. DocPlanner was founded in 2012 in Poland by Mariusz Gralewski, merged with Spain's Doctoralia in 2016, and launched its Brazilian operation in Curitiba in 2017. In Brazil, Doctoralia operates both a patient-facing appointment-booking marketplace and a SaaS suite (Doctoralia Pro) for individual doctors and clinics. In July 2022 it acquired Brazilian clinic-management SaaS **Feegow**, significantly expanding its B2B EHR and financial-management capabilities. DocPlanner reached unicorn status (≥US$1 billion valuation) in 2021 and has raised approximately €300 million in total. Among Brazilian platforms, Doctoralia is distinctive for combining a high-traffic patient marketplace with practitioner SaaS tools — a model not replicated by [[iclinic]], [[shosp]], [[clinica-nas-nuvens]], [[pixeon]], or [[mv-sistemas]]. See [[market-overview]] for competitive context.

## Founders

- **Mariusz Gralewski** — Founder and Global CEO, DocPlanner Group. The idea originated in 2010 when Gralewski struggled to find a vetted specialist doctor late at night; he purchased the ZnanyLekarz platform as the foundation. ## Key Features

### For Individual Health Professionals (Doctoralia Pro)
- Professional profile with Google-optimized visibility
- 24/7 online appointment booking for patients
- Automatic reminder and confirmation messages to reduce no-shows
- Electronic medical record (prontuário eletrônico) — extended via the Feegow acquisition
- Financial management tools
- Telemedicine / teleconsulta scheduling and video
- Patient communication tools
- ISO/IEC 27001 certified data security

### For Clinics
- All individual features above
- Multi-professional agenda management
- Advanced clinic management from the integrated Feegow platform
- Insurance/convênio billing support including anti-glosa tools (from Feegow)
- Marketing and patient-flow optimization

### Patient-Side Marketplace
- Search for doctors by specialty, location, and rating
- Patient reviews and doctor ratings
- Online booking 24/7
- 18 million+ unique visitors/month in Brazil (as of 2022)
- 700,000+ health professionals and clinics listed in Brazil
- Booking via WhatsApp, social networks, and Google My Business

## Target Market

Both **individual health professionals** (all specialties) and **clinics/hospitals**. Brazil marketplace reaches 18 million+ monthly unique visitors. DocPlanner Group has 280,000+ clients globally. The Brazilian operation focuses heavily on private-pay patients and health-plan patients seeking specialist appointments.

The dual marketplace-plus-SaaS model is Doctoralia's primary competitive moat: practitioners gain visibility in the marketplace in addition to practice-management tools, creating a network effect that pure SaaS competitors like [[iclinic]] and [[shosp]] replicate only partially via their own booking portals (AgendarConsulta and Shosp's online booking).
