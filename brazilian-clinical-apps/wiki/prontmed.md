Prontmed is a Brazilian electronic health record (EHR) platform founded in 1996 in São Paulo by **Wang Sen Feng**, built around specialty-specific clinical record templates designed by domain specialists rather than generalist software engineers. It serves 10,000+ health professionals daily, with 3 million consultations recorded and 10 million patients served. Its NPS is 65, with 75%+ of users rating it 9 or 10 out of 10. Notable institutional clients include **Hospital das Clínicas (HCUSP)**, **Sírio Libanês**, and **ECare** — placing it among the few Brazilian clinic platforms used by reference-tier teaching hospitals alongside [[medicina-direta]] and [[mv-sistemas]]. Its 2020 Series B — a strategic investment by **Fleury** (18%) and **Sabin** (12%), two of Brazil's largest diagnostic lab groups — was unusual in the sector and reflected the investors' interest in structured EHR data as an asset for diagnostic referral integration.

## Founders

- **Wang Sen Feng** — Founder and partner; re-assumed full executive control in December 2024 following the departure of external partners. Taiwanese-born; emigrated to São Paulo at age 14. IT specialization. Originally invited to build an EHR for the rheumatology department of **Hospital das Clínicas** — this project was the founding genesis of Prontmed. Wang also represents Brazil in **GO world championships**.
- **Lasse Koivisto** — Partner, executive (13 years, departed December 2024). Was previously an equity analyst at an asset manager. Met Wang Sen Feng at a **GO board game club**; in 2011 struck a deal exchanging free GO lessons for weekly business consulting.

Series B funds were also earmarked specifically for a **clinical decision support product** that cross-references EHR data with evidence-based protocols.

## Key Features

Prontmed structures its product around the consultation lifecycle:

The platform covers **33 specialties** with customized templates. Users report an average **10% reduction in appointment scheduling time**.

### Pré-consulta (Agenda)
- Customizable scheduling with per-consultation duration and room settings
- Independent profiles for physicians and secretaries (secretary profile is free)
- Intelligent agenda with automatic confirmation and 24-hour reminders
- Shortcut to next available appointment slots
- Internal team chat

### Momento da Consulta (Prontuário)
- Specialty-specific EHR templates created by specialists in each clinical field
- Timeline view of all prior consultations
- Drug interaction checking
- Validated medical scales across specialties
- Tables and graphs for tracking exam results over time
- Prescription: medications, exams, certificates, referrals, diets — with automation and database lookups
- Teleconsultation: patient video on same screen as record input
- Full patient event timeline

### Pós-consulta
- Digital prescriptions and exam orders automatically sent to patient with digital signature
- Patient-lab connection: scheduling exams at multiple partner laboratories
- [[tiss]] billing: batch creation, guide export in multiple formats

## Target Market

- Healthcare managers and administrators
- Health professionals across all specialties
- Clinics and consultórios to large hospital institutions
- Specialty society members (e.g., **SPGO** — Sociedade Paulista de Ginecologia e Obstetrícia — offers Prontmed **free** to member physicians)
- **Unimed Santos** (named institutional client)
