iClinic is Brazil's leading cloud-based clinic-management SaaS for independent physicians and small-to-medium clinics. Originally founded to give private-practice doctors the same technology available to large hospitals, it grew into a comprehensive platform covering scheduling, electronic health records (EHR), telemedicine, financial management, and patient marketing. The company was acquired by **Afya Limited** in January 2021 and now operates under the brand **Afya iClinic** as part of Afya's physician digital products division. Among Brazilian clinical platforms, it occupies the independent-practice segment — distinct from the enterprise hospital focus of [[pixeon]] and [[mv-sistemas]], and more feature-complete for solo practitioners than [[shosp]]'s free tier. See also the [[market-overview]] for competitive positioning.

## Founders

- **Felipe Lourenço** — Co-founder & CEO (through the acquisition); studied Informática Médica (Biomedical Informatics) at USP Ribeirão Preto. Post-acquisition, became Director of Digital Medical Products at Afya.
- **Leonardo Berdu** — Co-founder
- **Rafael Bouchabki** — Co-founder

All three founders are from Franca, SP, and have been friends since adolescence. In 2006 they moved to Ribeirão Preto to attend university, which set the stage for iClinic's headquarters there.

iClinic was the first Brazilian startup to enter the [Rockstart](https://rockstart.com) global accelerator program (Amsterdam, 2013). The team applied in December 2012, completed interviews in January 2013, and traveled to Amsterdam in February 2013. Rockstart selected iClinic from over 700 companies globally. The program comprised 5 months in Amsterdam followed by 5 months in San Francisco.

## Key Features

- **Prontuário Eletrônico (EHR):** Fully customizable per specialty; patient history, exam attachments, photo/image comparison, calculators, digital signature, electronic prescription (iClinic Rx) with up-to-date drug database delivered via WhatsApp
- **Agenda Médica:** Color-coded appointment types, auto-reminders (SMS, email, WhatsApp), waitlist management, recurring appointments, Google/Apple Calendar two-way sync, online booking via AgendarConsulta.com
- **Telemedicina (Teleconsulta):** Integrated video consultations with in-session EHR access, screen sharing, session recording, consent forms; unlimited on the Premium plan
- **Gestão Financeira:** Cash flow automation, expense/revenue control, [[tiss]]-standard health-plan billing guides, medical repasse, multi-bank accounts
- **Marketing Médico:** Email campaign tools, patient segmentation by age/gender/record tags, anniversary messages (Premium plan)
- **Controle de Estoque:** Inventory management integrated with the financial module
- **AgendarConsulta Marketplace:** Free professional profile on the patient-facing booking portal (comparable to [[doctoralia]]'s marketplace)
- **iClinic Rx:** Electronic prescription module with WhatsApp delivery

## Target Market

Independent physicians, dentists, psychologists, physiotherapists, and other health professionals. Supports 50+ specialties and serves professionals in cities across Brazil. Primary focus is the **independent private practice** (consultório/clínica) segment, not hospitals. iClinic self-describes as the **"first SaaS EHR in Brazil"**.

Unlike [[clinica-nas-nuvens]], which explicitly targets medium and large clinics, or [[mv-sistemas]] and [[pixeon]], which focus on hospitals and large institutions, iClinic's sweet spot is the solo or small-team practitioner.

## Pricing

Monthly subscription per health professional (per prestador). Secretaries and admin users are unlimited on all plans. 30-day free trial and 30-day money-back guarantee available.

| Plan | Monthly Price (BRL) | Highlights |
|------|--------------------|-|
| **Starter** | R$ 99 | Agenda, online booking, EHR, e-prescription, SMS/email reminders, reports |
| **Plus** | R$ 129 | Starter + appointment confirmations, internal chat, digital signature, WhatsApp reminders |
| **Pro** | R$ 169 | Plus + financial control, medical repasse, financial reports, inventory |
| **Premium** | R$ 299 | Pro + medical marketing, unlimited teleconsulta, Whitebook Premium access |

Source: [iclinic.com.br/sistema-medico](https://iclinic.com.br/sistema-medico/)

## Sales Model

iClinic relies primarily on **inside sales**. An inside sales function was added in 2015. By May 2020, a 20-person sales team was responsible for 85%+ of new sales. Average monthly ticket at that time was **R$ 100–110/month** per subscriber.

## Tech Stack

Pure cloud SaaS — no on-premise option. Accessible from any device via browser. Mobile apps available for iOS and Android. Specific underlying infrastructure (cloud provider, database technology) not publicly disclosed. LGPD-compliant data storage.

- **25,000** monthly active users
- **12,400** monthly paying subscribers
- **790,000+** medical consultations per month (of which **7,800** via telemedicine)
- **420,000+** electronic prescriptions per month
- **400,000+** image exams and lab tests prescribed per month
- **16 million** registered patients
- **11 million+** appointment reminders sent (cumulative platform milestone)

### COVID-19 Impact Note

iClinic had developed its telemedicine module in 2019 and launched it publicly in the second half of March 2020, immediately as pandemic-driven demand surged. By April 2020 the company had more than doubled its previous all-time monthly sales record, and telemedicine penetration within its existing subscriber base exceeded 60%.
