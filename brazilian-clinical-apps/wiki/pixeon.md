Pixeon is one of Brazil's leading healthcare IT companies, offering an integrated portfolio covering the full spectrum of hospital, clinic, and diagnostic-center software: HIS (Hospital Information Systems), RIS (Radiology Information Systems), PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication Systems), LIS (Laboratory Information Systems), and CIS (Clinical Information Systems). Founded in Florianópolis in 2003 (originally as **Pixeon Medical Systems**), Pixeon reached its current form through a series of mergers and acquisitions, most significantly a ~2012 union with Medical Systems. In September 2025, it announced it would join **Vela LatAm** (a subsidiary of Canada's **Constellation Software**, operating under the Vela Software division), pending CADE approval. Pixeon is the only large-scale EHR in Brazil certified by SBIS at the highest digital maturity level, and its PACS Aurora product has been voted Best PACS in Latin America by KLAS Research four consecutive times. It was also the first company to implement a digital imaging exam scheduling system in Brazil. Among the platforms in the [[market-overview]], Pixeon occupies the enterprise hospital and diagnostic-center tier alongside [[mv-sistemas]], and differs fundamentally in target and product from clinic-focused SaaS platforms like [[iclinic]], [[shosp]], [[clinica-nas-nuvens]], and [[easy-health]].

## Target Market

**Hospitals, large clinics, diagnostic centers, and laboratories** — primarily medium-to-large institutions. Serves 3,000+ institutions across Brazil, Argentina, Uruguay, Colombia, and Peru, including **530+ clinical care institutions** and **39 public health institutions**. Key clients include:

- **Hapvida** — processes 17M+ exams/year; described as the largest diagnostic imaging operation in Latin America; tripled imaging report output using Pixeon
- **Hospital Dona Helena** — achieved 70% reduction in exam rooms using Pixeon's digital solutions
- **Hospital Ana Nery** — 90% of exams digitized
- **LACEN-MG** — covers 853 municipalities

This enterprise focus mirrors [[mv-sistemas]] and contrasts sharply with clinic-focused platforms [[iclinic]], [[shosp]], [[clinica-nas-nuvens]], and [[easy-health]]. Pixeon's specific strength in PACS/RIS/LIS gives it a diagnostic-imaging niche that [[mv-sistemas]] also covers but through a different product architecture. See [[market-overview]] for segment comparison.
