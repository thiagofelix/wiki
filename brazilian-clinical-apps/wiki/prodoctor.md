ProDoctor (legal name: ProDoctor Software S/A) is one of Brazil's longest-running medical software companies, entering the market in **1993** from Juiz de Fora, Minas Gerais. It serves **65,000+ clients** across all Brazilian states, with a reported NPS of 97 for customer support. The platform offers tiered products ranging from simple consultório management (ProDoctor Cloud) to enterprise-level clinic and hospital systems (ProDoctor Corp), with AI-powered transcription via Voa, an open API, and [[tiss]]/TUSS compliance. ProDoctor's telemedicine solution was selected as an **AWS global case study**, distinguishing it technically among Brazilian clinic platforms. Its 30+ years of market continuity without disclosed external VC funding make it comparable in longevity to [[conclinica]] (1991) and [[hidoctor]] (Centralx, pre-2000).

## Product Tiers

Pre-cloud era metrics: 7,900+ installations, 40,000+ licensed users. **40,000+ users** were recorded as of July 2021 (the baseline at Jomar Nascimento's CEO appointment); the platform has since grown to **65,000+ clients**.
