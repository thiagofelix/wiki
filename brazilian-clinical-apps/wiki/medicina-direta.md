Medicina Direta is a Brazilian electronic health record (EHR) and clinic management platform developed by **Neodel Tecnologia e Software LTDA EPP**, based in São Paulo. Its primary differentiator in the Brazilian market is holding **SBIS Level 3 certification** — the highest level awarded by the Sociedade Brasileira de Informática em Saúde — which is required by institutions seeking ONA, Joint Commission, AACI, ACSA, and Qmentum accreditations. It is one of the very few Brazilian clinic platforms that explicitly serves both the private healthcare sector and the public **SUS** system, with dedicated forms for UBS, UPA, AMA, vaccination units, and mobile health units. Compare with [[feegow]] (SBIS NGS2 Level 2) and the majority of platforms that have no SBIS certification.

## Target Market

Medicina Direta serves a broader institutional range than most Brazilian clinic software:

**Private sector:**
- Consultórios (individual practices)
- Hospital Dia
- Unidades Odontológicas (dental)
- Policlínicas
- Pronto Atendimento (urgent care)
- Ambulatórios
- Reabilitação
- Hospital Escola

**Public sector (SUS):**
- UBS (Basic Health Units)
- UPA (Emergency Ambulatory Units)
- AMA
- Unidades de Vacinação
- Unidades Móveis
