Conclínica is the online clinic management SaaS platform developed by **Tecnoarte Tecnologia e Gestão em Saúde** (legal entity: Tecnoarte Informática Ltda. EPP, CNPJ: 01.399.304/0001-36), a Rio de Janeiro health technology and management consulting firm founded in 1991–1992. With 30+ years in health IT — placing it alongside [[prodoctor]] (1993) and [[hidoctor]] (Centralx, pre-2000) as one of the longest-operating companies in Brazilian clinic software — Tecnoarte's Conclínica product targets clinics and consultórios of all specialties. It offers transparent pricing at **R$ 79/user/month** (Premium plan), making it one of the few platforms with publicly listed rates alongside [[simdoctor]] and [[consultorio-live]]. Tecnoarte also maintains a separate enterprise product, **Visual ASA**, for larger hospital institutions.

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Multi Agenda** | Multi-professional, multi-specialty, multi-unit scheduling; supports order-of-arrival or appointment-based flow; full remote access |
| **Prontuário Eletrônico** | Cloud-stored EHR; per-specialty custom fields; access restricted by login/password |
| **Laudos e Exames Online** | Lab/exam result management; patient categories ("pendente", "digitado", "liberado"); patient portal for downloading results |
| **Faturamento TISS** | Online health plan authorization; [[tiss]] guide generation; batch export (up to 100 per convênio) |
| **Controle Financeiro** | Accounts payable/receivable; specialty/professional revenue views; glosa and repasse management; treasury control |
| **Controle de Estoque** | Item registration with unit of measure, reorder points, lot numbers, storage locations; movement tracking (entrada/saída/transferência/consumo/perdas) |
| **Cobrança Inteligente** | Subscriber/dependent billing; automated boleto via email; bank system integration; auto-reconciliation on payment |
| **Relatório Gerencial** | Real-time KPI dashboard; charts for scheduling, attendance, patient, billing, financial, and inventory data |
| **Agendamento Online** | Patient self-scheduling via internet |
| **Lembretes de Consulta** | Automated appointment reminders |
| **Armazenamento em Nuvem** | Cloud data storage with encryption |
| **Multi-Clinic Management** | Single platform managing 5+ clinics remotely (documented in customer testimonial from Dr. Pedro Baches) |

## Target Market

- Medical clinics (clínicas médicas)
- Dental clinics (clínicas odontológicas)
- Laboratories (laboratórios)
- Other health services
- Healthcare professionals: physicians, nurses, clinic managers

## Pricing

**Premium plan ("Mais vendido"):** R$ 79 per user/month — includes:
- Agenda Online
- Prontuário Eletrônico
- Faturamento (billing)
- Controle Financeiro
- Módulo de Laudos (exam/lab reports)
- Additional features

Free trial available at: https://app.conclinica.com.br/cliente/cadastro/novo
