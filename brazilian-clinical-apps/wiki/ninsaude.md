Ninsaúde is a Brazilian health-tech company that develops **Ninsaúde Clinic**, also marketed as the **Apolo** system (purchased via apolo.ninsaude.com). Founded in 2013, with the Clinic product launched in early 2018, the platform targets medical, dental, physiotherapy, aesthetics, and ASD/ABA treatment clinics, as well as **health franchise networks** — its primary differentiator in a market where most competitors, such as [[amplimed]], [[feegow]], and [[prodoctor]], do not offer dedicated franchise-scale multi-unit management. The platform also offers English and Spanish interfaces, targeting Brazilian health professionals operating internationally.

## Key Features

Ninsaúde Clinic is modular; clinics can activate the components they need:

| Module | Description |
|---|---|
| **Prontuário Eletrônico** | Legal-validity EHR; AI transcription (Ninsaúde Safe); customizable per specialty including odontogram for dental |
| **Telemedicina** | Unlimited teleconsultation integrated in the platform |
| **Agenda** | Multi-professional scheduling; day/week/month/professional views; room-based scheduling |
| **Agendamento Online** | Patient self-scheduling 24/7; embeds in clinic website or social media |
| **Confirmação WhatsApp** | Automated appointment confirmations via WhatsApp |
| **Faturamento de Convênios** | [[tiss]] 03.04.00 guide types: Consulta, SADT, Honorários; batch organization |
| **Ninsaúde Sign** | Integrated electronic and digital signature for consents and contracts |
| **Ninsaúde Pay** | Online payment link sent to patient via email or WhatsApp before consultation |
| **Ninsaúde Safe** | AI-powered consultation recording and transcription with legal protection |
| **CRM / Funil de Vendas** | Patient acquisition funnel and retention tools |
| **Financeiro** | Cash flow, DRE, bank reconciliation; package/subscription management; PIX, card, and boleto payment support |
| **Estoque** | Inventory management with lot numbers, expiry tracking, movement reports |
| **NFS-e** | Electronic service invoice issuance directly from the system |
| **E-mail Marketing** | Appointment reminders, satisfaction surveys, and marketing campaigns |
| **Pesquisa de Satisfação** | Patient satisfaction surveys |
| **Check-in do Paciente** | Patient check-in module |
| **API Aberta** | Open API for third-party integrations |
| **Customizable Layout** | Clinic can apply its own brand colors and layout to the system |
| **Franchise Management** | Multi-unit dashboards, DRE per unit, and franchise-scale reporting — primary differentiator |

## Target Market

- Medical, dental, physiotherapy, and aesthetics clinics
- **Health franchise networks** (primary differentiator vs. competitors)
- ASD/ABA treatment centers
- Multi-specialty clinics
- International market: English and Spanish interfaces available; clients include Brazilian professionals in the UK, USA, and Germany
