Feegow is a Brazilian clinic management platform (Practice Management System, PMS) founded in 2009 by Silvio Maia in Rio de Janeiro. It grew into one of Brazil's most feature-rich cloud-based clinic management solutions, marketing 200+ features across scheduling, electronic health records, financial management, health plan billing, and telemedicine. As of the DocPlanner Group acquisition in July 2022 the platform served 40,000+ healthcare professionals and 68,000+ total users. It is now operated under **DocPlanner Group** (Warsaw, Poland), which also owns [[tuotempo]] and the Brazilian marketplace brand **Doctoralia**. Feegow holds **SBIS NGS2 Level 2 certification** (highest security level), making it one of the few [[tiss]]-compliant platforms with independent security validation.

**DNA Capital** is a healthcare-focused global investment firm with offices in São Paulo and San Francisco. It led the Series A and a follow-on round before the DocPlanner exit. Total disclosed pre-acquisition funding: ~USD 4.06M (HealthTech Alpha estimate).

**DocPlanner Group** (Warsaw) acquired Feegow in July 2022 to integrate its PMS capabilities with the Doctoralia marketplace (18 million monthly users, 700,000+ listed professionals in Brazil). Post-acquisition, Feegow customers gained access to the Doctoralia marketplace; Doctoralia customers gained access to Feegow's EHR and financial tools.

DocPlanner Group itself was valued at USD 1 billion+ as of a September 2021 funding round led by Goldman Sachs.

## Key Features

Feegow markets 200+ features across five core modules:

| Module | Key Capabilities |
|---|---|
| **Agenda / Scheduling** | Multi-professional, multi-unit scheduling; no-show reduction guarantee; automated reminders |
| **Prontuário com IA** | AI-assisted electronic health record; auto-generates documentation during consultations |
| **Módulo Financeiro** | Full financial management: accounts payable/receivable, cash flow, reports |
| **Gestão de Convênios** | Health plan management with automatic [[tiss]] guide issuance |
| **Relatórios** | 100% customizable reports for agendas, revenues, and patients |
| **Telemedicina** | Teleconsultation support with integrated record |
| **Mobile App** | Android/iOS app for clinic management; integrated chat support |
| **Interoperability** | Native integrations and open API |

## Target Market
