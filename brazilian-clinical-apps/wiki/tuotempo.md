TuoTempo is an enterprise healthcare CRM and patient engagement platform founded in Italy (2005–2006) and acquired by **DocPlanner Group** in 2019. Unlike most Brazilian clinic software — such as [[amplimed]], [[feegow]], or [[prodoctor]] — which targets individual practices and small-to-medium clinics, TuoTempo is built specifically for hospitals, polyclinics, diagnostic centers, and health insurance companies. It orchestrates the entire patient journey through omnichannel appointment booking, automated multi-channel communications, recall campaigns, online payments, and predictive analytics. Within the DocPlanner Group umbrella, it operates alongside [[feegow]] (Brazilian PMS) and the Doctoralia marketplace.

## Key Features

TuoTempo specializes in patient relationship management and operational automation for large healthcare institutions:

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Omnichannel Appointment Booking** | Online/mobile appointment booking for patients; embeds into institutional websites and apps |
| **Automated Reminders** | Multi-channel: SMS, email, push notifications, automated voice calls — for appointment confirmations |
| **Double Confirmation** | Configurable double-confirmation workflow to reduce no-shows |
| **Personalized Reminders** | Patient- and service-specific reminder timing and channel optimization |
| **Recall Campaigns** | Automated recall and follow-up campaigns by treatment type |
| **Post-Appointment Satisfaction Surveys** | Automated patient satisfaction data collection |
| **Wait-List Management** | Cancellation-based wait list with automatic backfill |
| **Online Documents** | Patients download reports, diagnostic images, and invoices 24/7 from mobile or desktop |
| **Online Payment** | PayPal-integrated online payments; refunds and discounts processing |
| **Marketing Analytics** | Campaign performance analysis; A/B testing for reminder timing |
| **Healthcare CRM** | Audience targeting, campaign management, marketing automation, medical history records |
| **Patient Communications** | Multi-channel patient messaging management |
| **Predictive Analytics** | Forecasting patient behavior and cancellation risk |
| **Multi-Location** | Full support for hospital networks and polyclinics with multiple locations |
| **API** | Open API for integration with HIS/EMR systems |

## Target Market

TuoTempo targets large, institutional healthcare providers — not individual practitioners or small clinics:

- Hospitals and polyclinics
- Diagnostic centers and imaging facilities
- Health insurance companies
- European and Latin American healthcare institutions

This contrasts sharply with the individual-practice and small-clinic focus of [[feegow]], [[amplimed]], [[simdoctor]], and [[consultorio-live]].
